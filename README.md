## 🧠 Who Am I

I'm **Ibai Fernándex** — a Spanish developer living between Bogotá and the future.

I don't just write code. I **orchestrate AI** to build things that matter: automations, platforms, pipelines, and products that work while I sleep. My philosophy is simple:

> *Why do it manually when a system can do it for you?*

I wear three hats right now:

- 🎯 **Mail Marketing & Automation Director** at [LFi](https://lfi.agency) — architecting multi-channel campaigns that convert
- ⚡ **Founder of [AGLAYA](https://aglaya.agency)** — *The Uncomfortable AI·gency*. We automate what others still do by hand
- 💻 **Vibe-Coder** — building full-stack apps by conducting AI like an orchestra
